My website has been in existence in some form or another for the past decade at least. Years ago when I was on more social media sites I'd have my profile as "| human being | catholic priest | artist |" which is funny to me now in the age of AI, guess it's good to advertise I'm actually a human being. But anyway, that's the stuff on this site, human things like writings and art, and it seems the more years of priesthood I get under my belt, most of those writings start to sound like sermons.

This website is hand written html and txt files. Bare bones, inspect the source, anyone could put something together like this. Text is best.

I did finally cave and add an rss feed, it'll contain the three most recent additions to the site.

Everything I've written on this webpage is within the public domain.
